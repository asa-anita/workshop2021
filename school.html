ANITA summer school program - "Green computing"

Green computing

The 2021 ANITA "summer" school on "Green Computing" will take place over three sequential Thursdays, July 3, 10, and 17. We will meet between 1 PM and 4:30 PM AEST via zoom (with thanks to Astro3D for zoom support).

The first meeting, on June 3, will include an introduction to computing resources from ADACS coordinator Greg Poole and ASTAC chair Bernhard Mueller; a talk from Adam Stevens, lead author of Nature Astronomy article "The imperative to reduce carbon emissions in astronomy"; and a panel discussion between Adam, Vanessa Moss, Pascal Elahi and Natasha Hurley-Walker on the same topic.
