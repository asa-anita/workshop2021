ANITA 2021 workshop and `Green computing' summer school

The 15th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held on 18th-19th of February 2021 at Macquarie University in Sydney. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here.

It will be preceded by the ANITA summer school - "Green computing" on 15th-17th of February 2021, which will also occur at AAO / Macquarie.

ANITA Workshop 2021

The 2021 ANITA science workshop will be held on the 18th-19th of February at Macquarie.

ANITA 2021 school

The 2021 ANITA summer school will run 15th-17th February at the same location.

The theme is "Green computing".
