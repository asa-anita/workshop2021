ANITA 2021 workshop and `Green computing' summer school

The 15th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held via zoom over several consecutive weeks, with two sessions per week, noon-2PM AEDT on Monday and Friday. The meeting will start on Monday, February 8. The exact number of sessions to be determined based on the number of talk submissions. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here. As always, early career researchers are particularly encouraged to contribute talks.

The ANITA summer school on "Green computing" will be announced at a later date.

ANITA Workshop 2021

The 2021 ANITA science workshop will take place via zoom starting 8 February, 2021.
